About Our Rig

Our adventure vehicle is a 2010 Toyota 4Runner which we have affectionately named Balerion the Black Dread (B for short) after a dragon from the Game of Thrones universe. We did our best to keep the vehicle as close to stock (and lightweight) as possible to keep it as reliable and fuel efficient as we could. The only performance upgrades we did was adding a set of Bilstein 5100 shocks, stiffer Eibach springs to the rear, a front skid plate from RCI, set of five Falken Wildpeak tires, and a fifth matching rim so that we can rotate the spare in regularly.

For living and comfort we added a full length Prinsu rack and an Autohome Columbus Variant tent. On the inside we started with a full length Goose Gear platform and bolted on a kitchen from Overland Kitchen as well as a closet built by Andy's dad. After being on the road for a while we decided to bite the bullet and install a WeBoost which has really helped our ability to stay in touch with family and friends when we are traveling remotely.

If you have any questions we didn't answer feel free to drop us a line!
