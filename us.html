About Andy

Well hello there; I'm Andy! I had originally dreamed of doing the Pan American Highway (or PAH as we affectionately call it) around 2012. As a kid I did a lot of camping (shout out to the Red Wing ELC) and my parents took us on a few trips where we were only allowed a small duffel bag each. All of this is likely what led me to ultimately want to live in a car with Nadeesha while bopping around North and South America.

About Nadeesha

I'm originally from Sri Lanka; I came to the states for college and stuck around to hang out with Andy. I'm somewhat new to the outdoors, but ever so grateful for discovering how wonderful it is. In my 20's I didn't think I'd steer away from the corporate career path, but now I'm a part time corporate job holder and full-time overlander!!! It's exciting to see what the future holds as we continue our travels south to Ushuaia!

About Us

We started dating back in 2011 and got married in the fall of 2012 (Andy wasn’t wasting any time). Our first anniversary trip was also Nadeesha’s first backpacking trip and (much to Andy’s surprise) she had a blast! After that we started taking regular camping trips with friends and by ourselves. One of the many memorable experiences was a multi-day trip to the Havasupai Reservation in Arizona with several friends.

Some time around 2018 we began seriously discussing doing the Pan American and in 2020 we sold our house and downsized to a much less expensive one to begin saving aggressively for the trip. Then, in the spring of 2021 we hit the road! Follow along on Instagram to see what kind of shenanigans we get into!

If you have any questions we didn't answer feel free to drop us a line!
